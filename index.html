Sub-Divisions

Our team consists of four Sub-divisions, namely: Mechanical, Avionics, Control & System & Business.

Sub-Divisions

Mechanical

The mechanical subdivision is responsible for planning, building and testing all the parts of the Vtol.

Avionics

The electrical sub-division is responsible for managing the power requirements, controlling thrusters and actuation systems.

Control & Software

The control & Software Subdivision is responsible for making RaptorX Autonomous!

Business

The Business Subdivision handles all things non-tech including Events, Design, Marketing, Media, PR and Hr Web form its backbone.
